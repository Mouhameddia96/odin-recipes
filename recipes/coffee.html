Cup of Coffee

This coffee is a simple, yet satisfying hot beverage. Made from freshly brewed coffee beans or ground coffee, it boasts a rich, aromatic flavor. It can be customized with optional milk or cream for a smoother taste, and sugar for sweetness. Perfect for a morning pick-me-up or a cozy evening treat.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Coffee beans (or pre-ground coffee)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons coffee per 6 ounces water into your coffee maker or French press.
  3. OBrew (4-5 min for French press).
  4. Serve hot, adjust with optional milk and sugar as desired.
